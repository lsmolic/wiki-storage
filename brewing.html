Brewing

Sake is a brewed beverage. That means it is far similar to beer than it is to wine, with which it is often confused. In fact there is an entirely different product called rice wine that is used in lots of Asian cuisine, but if you drink that you'll quickly see how it differs from the incredible drink we call Sake.
